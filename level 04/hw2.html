The 2024–2026 Mercedes-AMG C 63 S E Performance is a high-performance plug-in hybrid luxury sedan/estate featuring a 671 hp (combined) 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine and rear electric motor. It achieves 0–60 mph in 3.3 seconds, with 752 lb-ft of torque, standard 4MATIC+ AWD, and 2.5-degree rear-axle steering.
