Willie wonkas factory

Willy Wonka's factory is a legendary, secretive chocolate production
facility from Roald Dahl’s Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.
It features a magical, candy-filled landscape, including a chocolate river, waterfall,
The Inventing Room, and the Television Room. Operated by Oompa Loompas, it is designed with fantastical,
often dangerous, inventions, serving as both a,factory and the home of Willy Wonka.
